Built Weekender, a polished responsive trip-planning app with:

- Destination comparison cards for Lisbon, Copenhagen, and Lake Como
- Voting interactions with live vote counts
- Budget slider and travel-time details
- Group vote visualization
- Lisbon itinerary preview with expandable details
- Responsive desktop sidebar and mobile navigation
- Realistic sample data and polished visual styling
