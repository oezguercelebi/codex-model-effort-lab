How to read this

Your eyes are the evaluator.

Visual quality is not reduced to a synthetic score. Engineering reviews inspect source and behavior; your eyes still judge design, product decisions, and whether the result feels finished.

  • Look for hierarchy, polish, useful states, and coherent decisions.
  • Then check whether extra time and tokens created visible value.
  • Remember one run is evidence, not a universal model ranking.
